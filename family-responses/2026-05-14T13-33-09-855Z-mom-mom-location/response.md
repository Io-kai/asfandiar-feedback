## Answer

I was in my own house when the soldiers were surrounding my house.I didn't know at the time because I was by myself in the house. I wasn't aware that soldiers were surrounding me and that there were soldiers up on top of the roof. 

Then one of my friends came over; her name is Runak Malakurda. She was such a nice lady. I was quite young at that time when she came in and told me, "Nigar, your house is surrounded by tanks and soldiers on the roof."

I asked why, and she said it was because my husband, Asfandiar, was gone to the mountains. Actually, I wasn't that scared, but Runak was more scared than I was. She was smart, and she asked me where my abaya was (an abaya is a black cloth you use to cover yourself). She said, "When we go out from your house, let's go to my house. Tell them you are a cleaning lady." 

I said okay. I put on the abaya and we went out. A soldier asked me in Arabic, "What are you doing here? Who are you?" 

I told him, "I'm a cleaning lady. I just finished cleaning and I'm leaving." 

Thank God, I was safe. If they had known I was his wife, they would have taken me and killed me, or perhaps taken me as a hostage until my husband returned. But this lady saved me. From there, I went and hid in my mother's house.
