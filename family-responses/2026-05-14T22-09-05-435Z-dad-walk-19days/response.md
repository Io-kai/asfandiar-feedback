## Question

On the 1963 19-day walk home, what is the hardest moment you remember most clearly?

## Answer

It was very difficult in those days to walk through these rugged mountains with shoes which were not made for the terrain. I wore dress shoes, but still I tried my best and I did it, although I was very, very tired after each day of walking with no food, no shelter, and no guides. 

The road you are on, nobody else was. I had two other people with me who were also like me; they didn't know where we were going.
