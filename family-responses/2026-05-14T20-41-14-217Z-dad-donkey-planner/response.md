## Answer

The arranged donkey assaults were planned by the head of Peshmerga in the area. The donkeys belonged to different villages around the area, specifically around Handrin and Zozek mountains.At the time, I was a lieutenant physician captain in the area with the 4th Iraqi Army Division. The plan for the assault on our garrison was delivered; after I saw the plan, I made copies of it and traveled back to Sulaymaniyah.

I gave it to the head of the Peshmerga to deliver to Fakher Mergasuri, who was at the time the head of the assault area in Handrin Mountain. At the time of the assault, when the army drew back and many poor soldiers were killed, he sent somebody to make sure I was still alive.
