## Question

Before you reached Asfandiar, did you walk for hours, walk for days, ride in a car, or some combination?

## Answer

I see. You know, when I went walking on the mountain for probably 12-14 hours with no ride, car, or anything, for some of the time (probably half an hour or 45 minutes) I rode a mule. I talked to one of the people who were transferring goods to the other villages on mules. 

I asked the first man, but he said, "No ma'am, I have a big, heavy load; I cannot put you on that." He left, and I was so disappointed. 

Then another one came and I begged him. I said, "I'm skinny. Will you please give me a ride on that mule for a while? I'm very tired." Fortunately, he said yes. It was good; I was so happy to have some rest.
