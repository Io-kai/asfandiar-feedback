## Question

Did you hide at your mother's house, Asfandiar's house, a step-brother's house in Baghdad, or more than one place at different times?

## Answer

First, I hid at my mom's house for probably a month. I was so scared because I knew they were looking for me and wanted to find me; I was constantly afraid they would find me there.

Then I went to my friend's house for a couple of days, but I couldn't stand being anywhere. After that, I went to my stepbrother's house in Baghdad. I took my ring off when I traveled. We took a taxi (a car) over there. I was kind of scared there too, because it wasn't my house and I wasn't used to it, but they were nice to me.

I stayed there for a couple of months. I was always asking my mom if he had sent me a note or something so I could come back. I wanted to come back. I didn't even care if they were going to kill me, because I was not comfortable there either.
